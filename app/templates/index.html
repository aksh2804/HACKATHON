SMART THAPAR HACKATHON

Brought to you by Creative Computing Society


Development is more of an adrenaline rush than a mere feeling. Computer world today is a race between software engineers striving to build bigger and better, and the demand of this modernistic era is to explore the innovative corners along with building solutions that are easy-to-use. If you think you have an idea that you can share, then this is your chance to show the world your idea. So, come along as HELIX brings you a 24-Hour hackathon- SMART THAPAR HACKATHON brought to you by CREATIVE COMPUTING SOCIETY.

Inspired from SMART INDIA HACKATHON(SIH), CCS brings to you a hackathon of this kind- SMART THAPAR HACKATHON, where teams have to conceptualize a problem statement or issues from different societies of Thapar, develop a strategy and share their masterplan. CCS has collaborated with different societies of Thapar to come up with a potential solution to their technical problems.

RULEBOOK

Rules and regulations

  • Problem statements and issues from different societies of Thapar will be present on this website. So, the participants are requested to choose the problem statement of their choice beforehand.
  • Participants need to come in a group of 3 or 4 to team up against the challenge.
  • Once the hackathon commences there will be mid term evaluations and participants will be guided by the judging panel.
  • All the entries will be reviewed by the judging panel based on design, fulfillment of the statement, user friendliness and uniqueness.
  • Judging of entries and its acceptance of portal or app by the problem statement provider will be an independent decision.
  • If the solution is accepted by the problem statement provider then the team will get a certificate regarding their project by the concerned society as well as CCS itself.
  • The event will have three winning criteria:- Overall Winner, Best Overall First Year team, Best all girls team.

    PRATIGYA

    Har Hath Kalam

    (1) CHILD MONITORING SYSTEM

    Har Hath Kalam India Association requires a web portal to track the performance of all the underprivileged kids who were sent to school by the organization, which will be further integrated with the website www.harhathkalam.org. This portal will be a medium of communication between the school faculty and HHK to monitor the attendance and academic performance of the kid. The portal will have two types of users. The school faculty or authorized HHK volunteer, any one of them will upload the initial details (name, class, address, roll no. and parents’ names etc.) of the kid and the school staff will update the attendance and marks of each quarterly exam regularly. This portal will be used by more than a hundred schools and details of thousands of kids will be entered in the nearby future so the database of the portal must be designed and optimized accordingly. The viewer must be able to view the data section wise, class wise, school wise also.

It must include an easy to use the feature where HHK representatives can upload news and events regularly on the App. The link to App is : https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.harhathkalam.hhk.kalam

    Thapar Amateur Astronomers Society (TAAS)

    Thapar Amateur Astronomers Society (TAAS) is a student society in TIET, Patiala. It needs an app to check the management and functioning of society. The app must have following features:

    1. Every society member will be able to use app using a login ID and password.
    2. There must be an admin who shall be able to add/remove members for having an access to the app. Admin can also add other Admin to the app. There can be multiple Admins.
    3. Admin and other members will have different levels of accessibility to app.

    Following tabs shall be required in the app.

    1. Attendance: - Accessibility:
      • Members will be able to check their own attendance only. If doubtful, they can raise an enquiry which will notify admin about same.
      • Admin will be able to edit attendance in terms of
      - Adding a date of attendance.
      - Editing the by default 'Absent' field to 'Present' or 'Leave' by his own discretion.
    2. Event and Duties:
      • This will contain Bullet points for each Event with the event date displayed with it.
      • Each event bullet will contain sub-bullets for smaller parts of the event.
      • Each sub-bullet will be having further to-do task list with a member assigned for who will be doing each task. Only the particular member who is assigned task (+ Admin) can edit the status of task from Undone/Done.
      • Each task will have a Last Date. App will set reminder for each member according to their task and will send notifications to member and admins.
      • While changing the task's status to 'Done', person will need to upload a text/picture/document for verification.
      • If Done in time, Task's name will change colour to green, and Red otherwise.
      • On Admin's discretion, Event will be rendered completed, after which, no edits can be done in it by anyone except if it's opened again for edits.
      • Accessibility:
      - Admin: Add/Remove/Edit Event, Dates, Sub-events, Tasks, assigned members, last date etc.
      - Member: See all the events, sub-event, tasks, able to edit task status they were assigned.
      Raise query/question about event/sub-event/task/date etc, which will send notification to admins.
    3. Documents/Links etc.
      • Events in bullet points like in 2nd Tab
      • Whenever event is created by admin, under this section, following sub-bullets will be created by default namely
      - Proposal
      - Indent
      - Report
      - Forwarding Memo
      - Recredit application
      - Bills
      - Bookings
      - Permissions
      - Events Pictures
      - Always compulsory to fill before declaring event complete
      - If one is compulsory, all others are compulsory.
      - Only compulsory if specified while creating event.
      • Each of the sub-bullet will contain relevant documents in them, in form of pictures/documents/excel/link etc.
      • Accessibility
      - Admins can edit/add/remove documents in all categories and change the compulsion status, if required.
      - Members can view all the documents except Indent/Forwarding Memo.
    4. Personal Score:
      This will have 3 bullets.
      1. Your Karma
      2. Self-effort notification
      3. Bonus
      • Each member can view their personal score in 1st bullet which will be decided according to:
      Attendance (Present)5 + Absent-5 +Leave*0 + Task Completed on time*15 + Task completed with delay*5 + Task not completed* -10 + Self-effort points (as given by admin) + Bonus Points
      • Self-effort Notification:
      Members can send a notification to admins about something which they did except their assigned task ( in form of text/picture). Admin will check and approve that how much points member deserves from this.
      • Bonus:
      Admin's, on their discretion can allot bonus points to members.
    5. Your Account:
      - Like General apps, members can edit their own information, display picture etc.
